Hi all, Micrograam has been entered for the Sankalp award 2014. Your vote can help Micrograam win this award. You can cast your vote every 24 hours to ensure your chosen organization wins. For some of you who do not know about Micrograam: Micrograam helps in raising money for people from the lowest income groups and empowers them to lead a better life. Micrograam uses a ‘people invest in people’ model. In this model, people like you and me can invest money as low as Rs. 100 towards our chosen borrowers. The money is treated as a loan and repaid with interest. The repayment rate is as high as 99%. About Sankalp awards: Sankalp Forum, recognizes & supports innovative, sustainable, high-impact social enterprises. The Sankalp awards are India’s most prestigious Social Enterprise awards. Vetted through a rigorous 3-month process by a panel of global investors, the Awards bring recognition to the most sustainable and scalable social enterprises that are doing business across India.
